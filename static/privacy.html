Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  This policy describes how CS2CaseBot collects, uses, and protects your information.

📋 Summary: We collect only what we need to run the platform — your Discord ID, username, and avatar. We do not sell your data. We do not collect payment information directly. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

1. WHO WE ARE

CS2CaseBot ("we", "us", "the Platform") is an entertainment simulation platform. For privacy-related inquiries, contact us via our Discord Support Server.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect the following categories of information:

Data Type Source Purpose Retention
Discord User ID Discord OAuth2 Account identification Until account deletion
Discord Username Discord OAuth2 Display name in-platform Until account deletion
Discord Avatar Hash Discord OAuth2 Profile picture display Until account deletion
Session Token Generated on login Keep you logged in (7-day TTL) 7 days or until logout
Game Activity & History Platform interactions Statistics, achievements, leaderboards Until account deletion
Virtual Inventory Platform interactions In-platform item tracking Until account deletion
IP Address (logs) Server access logs Security & abuse prevention 30 days (automatic deletion)

We do not collect:

  • Real names or physical addresses
  • Payment card or banking information (handled by third-party processors)
  • Email addresses (unless voluntarily provided for support)
  • Device fingerprints or persistent tracking identifiers beyond the session cookie

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your logged-in session
  • Display your username and avatar within the Platform
  • Track your virtual balance, inventory, achievements, and game history
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and prevent abuse or fraud
  • Improve Platform performance and fix bugs
  • Display leaderboards (username and public stats only)

4. COOKIES & LOCAL STORAGE

We use the following cookies and local storage:

  • session_token (HTTP-only cookie) — required for authentication. Cannot be accessed by JavaScript. Expires after 7 days.
  • cookiesAccepted (localStorage) — records your cookie consent preference.
  • userSettings (localStorage) — stores your in-platform preferences (theme, sound, etc.) locally on your device.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies.

5. DATA SHARING

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Discord — solely to authenticate your identity via OAuth2. Governed by Discord's Privacy Policy.
  • Hosting & Infrastructure Providers — our servers and database providers process data to deliver the service. These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Law Enforcement — if required by applicable law, court order, or to protect the safety of our users.

6. DATA SECURITY

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

  • Session tokens stored as HTTP-only, Secure, SameSite cookies
  • Database access restricted to application servers only
  • HTTPS enforced for all connections
  • Session data stored in Redis with automatic 7-day expiry

No system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet.

7. DATA RETENTION

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days. Game logs may be retained in anonymised/aggregated form for platform analytics.

8. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction — request that inaccurate data be corrected
  • Right to Deletion — request that your account and data be deleted
  • Right to Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — you may stop using the Platform at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us via our Discord Support Server with the subject "Privacy Request".

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

CS2CaseBot is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us immediately and we will delete it.

10. INTERNATIONAL USERS

If you access the Platform from outside the country where our servers are located, your data may be transferred internationally. By using the Platform, you consent to such transfers. We take steps to ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection law.

11. THIRD-PARTY LINKS

The Platform may contain links to third-party websites (such as Discord). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

12. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Platform after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. CONTACT US

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us via our Discord Support Server. We aim to respond to all privacy requests within 30 days.
